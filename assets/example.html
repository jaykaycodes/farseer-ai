ARB SUMMIT BUMPER

The ARB Summit bumper for the 2019 Ranger continues the tradition of pioneering innovation with a relentless pursuit of quality. Features include press form cover straps, larger ARB fog lights, surround, and a three-piece split pan grille with access doors for winch clutch operation. It also includes a recessed winch control box mount for a sleek, integrated appearance.

The Ranger Summit Bumper is finished in ARB’s textured black Integrit™, offering class-leading protection and unparalleled corrosion resistance with an ultra-low sheen matte finish.

PROTECTION AND STYLING CHARACTERISTICS

  • Registered design of styling and design components
  • Durable steel construction for maximum protection
  • Bumper adds approximately 7” to the length of the vehicle
  • Approximate weight of the bumper, chassis mounts and fitting kit is 174lbs
  • Approximately 40lbs is removed from the vehicle for fitment
  • Engineered, airbag compatible mounting system secures to the chassis via high tensile bolts
  • Formed cover strap
  • Pressed form top pan and aluminum (powder-coated black) winch cover panel
  • Provision to accommodate ARB Intensity LED’s and a range of IPF lights
  • Incorporates ARB’s renowned multi fold upswept and tapered wing design for optimum strength and approach angle

FEATURES

CB Antenna Brackets

The Summit Bumper incorporates two CB antenna brackets welded behind the top cross tube. Custom fit, clamp on brackets are also available for additional antenna mounting.

CB Antenna Brackets

The Summit Bumper incorporates two CB antenna brackets welded behind the top cross tube. Custom fit, clamp on brackets are also available for additional antenna mounting.

Larger Tubing

60.3mm mandrel steel tubing provides increased frontal protection, as well offering greater visual styling to modern four-wheel-drive vehicles.

Wing Design

60.3mm mandrel steel tubing provides increased frontal protection, as well offering greater visual styling to modern four-wheel-drive vehicles.

Wider Edge Radius

A 30mm wing and center section edge radius offers increased strength while complementing modern vehicle design.

Driving Light Provision

Exceptionally durable and secure, all ARB Bumpers serve as an ideal platform to mount driving lights, helping ensure maximum performance and longevity. Driving lights mounted to a high-quality bumper prevents problems such as excessive movement, a vibrating beam pattern, premature bulb failure and eventual fatiguing of the light bodies.

Press Formed Top-Pan and Cover Panel

Press forming has been utilized to create a stronger center pan that features smooth, continuous edges. A press-formed aluminum winch cover panel finishes off the bumper for when a winch is not installed.

Jacking Points

Eliminating the need for a mounting bracket, jacking points are integrated into the design of Summit Bumpers.

Winch Compatibility

Mounting a winch to a vehicle requires a properly supported structure that is designed to accommodate the stresses of winching. When using a snatch block to increase winching capacity, the forces are multiplied to several times the rated capacity of the winch. The ARB Summit Bumper features the option of fitting a winch, and is designed and tested to endure and exceed such loads.

Split-Pan Design

The wide, split-pan design enhances the strength of the bumper, while providing the radiator and engine with a massive amount of air flow. An integrated winch clutch opening makes winch operation simple.

Air Bag Compatible

With an air bag equipped vehicle, it is essential that the vehicle's crush rate and air bag triggering are not altered when a bumper is installed. ARB assesses each vehicle's frontal crush characteristics and replicates the crush rate into the design of each air bag compatible bumper and its steel mounting system. This achieves maximum possible vehicle and passenger protection without affecting the crash pulse.

Fog Lights

Perfect for driving in inclement weather, these fog lights provide a broad spread of light to illuminate the road ahead.

LED Indicators and Clearance Lights

12 high intensity yellow LEDs and 4 white LEDs provide increased light output in all conditions.

Steel Uprights

Each bumper upright is cut from a single sheet of steel and extends from the top tube through to the bumper section and actually forms part of the bumper to chassis mount. This process ensures the whole outer frame receives the maximum possible support. Wide, press formed cover straps are then welded onto each upright to ensure there are no exposed sharp edges.
